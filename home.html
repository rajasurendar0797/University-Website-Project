    Cooking for Health and Happines

    One thing I always tell my clients is to make cooking and eating a mindful practice. When you’re present, cooking can feel meditative, and eating without distractions lets you appreciate every flavor and texture. It’s a small change that can make meals feel more satisfying and enjoyable. I often find peace in my kitchen, chopping vegetables and focusing solely on the colors and scents that surround me.
