    • Brazilian Pão de Queijo (Cheese Bread)

    Brazilian South American Breakfast Snack Vegetarian Gluten-Free South America Savory Comforting Quick recipes

    Ingredients

    • 250g tapioca flour
    • 100ml milk
    • 50ml oil
    • 2 eggs
    • 150g grated cheese
    • Salt to taste

    Instructions

    1. Preheat the Oven:
      • Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper for easy cleanup.
    2. Heat the Milk and Oil:
      • In a saucepan, heat the milk and oil over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it starts to simmer. Do not let it boil.
    3. Mix with Tapioca Flour:
      • Pour the hot milk and oil mixture over the tapioca flour in a large mixing bowl. Stir well to combine until you have a smooth, thick paste.
    4. Add Eggs, Cheese, and Salt:
      • Beat the eggs lightly and add them to the tapioca mixture. Stir until fully incorporated. Then, add the grated cheese and a pinch of salt, mixing everything together until smooth.
    5. Shape the Dough:
      • Using your hands or a spoon, shape the dough into small balls, about the size of a golf ball. Place them on the prepared baking sheet, spaced about 2 inches apart.
    6. Bake the Cheese Bread:
      • Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese breads are puffed up and golden brown on top.
    7. Serve:
      • Once baked, remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. These Brazilian cheese breads are best enjoyed warm!
